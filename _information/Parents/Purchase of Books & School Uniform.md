Year-End Purchase of Books & School Uniform 
--------------------------------------------

  

For the safety of our students and staff, the year-end purchase of books and school uniform is through **EITHER** online purchase **OR** physical purchase in the school.

**Online Purchase**

We strongly encourage our parents to purchase their child’s books and school uniform (with home delivery) via the vendor’s online portal [**www.asencio.com.sg**](http://www.asencio.com.sg/). The portal will be opened from **7 Nov 2022 to 14 Dec 2022** (2023 Sec 2- Sec 4 students).

  

For 2023 Secondary 1 students, online purchase is available from **19** **December** **to 24 December 2022.**

**Physical Purchase in School**

To ensure your safety and well-being, only one parent is allowed to accompany your child for walk-in purchase at the school bookshop.
