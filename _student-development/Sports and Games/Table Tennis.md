<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>A team that empowers every player to pursue&nbsp;<strong>sporting excellence</strong>&nbsp;with
unwavering&nbsp;<strong>courage</strong>, building the&nbsp;<strong>resilience</strong>&nbsp;to
overcome any challenge and the&nbsp;<strong>optimism</strong>&nbsp;to see
every match as an opportunity for growth.</p>
<p><strong>About Us</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>S6 Centre-Based Table Tennis CCA</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Strategic Cluster Initiative:</strong>&nbsp;A collaboration across
four schools to provide a wider variety of sporting options and promote
inter-school synergy<strong> </strong>that focuses on the positive energy
created by the partnership.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Participating Schools:</strong>&nbsp;Formed in 2024, by selected
students from&nbsp;<a href="https://www.bartleysec.moe.edu.sg/our-holistic-curriculum/co-curricular-activities/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Bartley Secondary</a>,&nbsp;
<a href="https://www.peicaisec.moe.edu.sg/cca/ccas/sports/tennis-cluster-based-interschool-cca/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Peicai Secondary</a>, St. Gabriel’s Secondary, and&nbsp;<a href="https://www.yuyingsec.moe.edu.sg/centre-based-table-tennis-cca/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Yuying Secondary</a>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Core Objectives:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Develop good skills and sportsmanship in students for both recreational
play and competitive excellence.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build strong network and teamwork between students from different school
backgrounds.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To establish a strong and consistent representation at the&nbsp;<a href="https://nsg.moe.edu.sg/sssc/table-tennis" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">National Schools Games (NSG)</a>.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
