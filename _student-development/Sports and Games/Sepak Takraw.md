<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>A team that believes in working hard with a never say die attitude in
becoming the best whilst showing class and sportsmanship at all times.</p>
<p><strong>Objective</strong> 
<br>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To improve the skills of the students.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select students to represent the school at the National Sports Games Sepak
Takraw ‘B’ and ‘C’ Division Competition.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To build the students’ character in the school values of Courage, Optimism,
Respect and Excellence</p>
</li>
</ol>
