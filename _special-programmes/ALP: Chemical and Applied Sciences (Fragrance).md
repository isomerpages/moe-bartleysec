<h3>Aim of the Programme</h3>
<p>Our ALP aims to prepare Bartleyans to become Learners for Life by:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Offering Bartleyans additional opportunities to learn through exploration
and discovery, thereby enabling invention and innovation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enthusing Bartleyans to explore the area of Science, Technology, Engineering
and Mathematics (STEM)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cultivating the habit of learning</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><em>Approach</em></strong> 
<br><strong>Broad-based Opportunities (Secondary 1 and 2)</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Secondary 1</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Acquire fundamental concepts about fragrances.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Perform separation techniques to extract essential oils from flowers,
spices and fruits</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use CAD software to create designs for 3D printing</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Execute Micro:bits coding to complete assigned tasks</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Secondary 2</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Acquire and apply concepts in formulation of perfumes</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Deepen understanding and application of CAD design and Micro:bits coding</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Implement Design-thinking Process,EDIPT (Empathy-Define-Ideate-Prototype-Test)
to create a prototype that caters to the theme specified by infusing the
concepts learnt</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><em>Programmes for Students with Higher Interest and Ability (Secondary 3 and above)</em></strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Partake in STEM-related competitions and enrichment activities</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in study-attachments/job shadowing/ learning journeys with
Educational and Industrial partners</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Take up Advanced Elective Module (AEM) in Singapore Polytechnic on Perfumery
and Cosmetic Science</p>
</li>
</ul>

</p>
