<p>1. The results of the 2025 GCE O Levels results would be released on 14
Jan 2026 at 2.00pm.</p>
<p>2. Students can report to the school by 1.30 pm to collect their results.
Please take note that students are <strong>required to report to school via the main gate</strong>.
There will be a <strong>briefing </strong>on admission exercises and registration
matters for all students <strong>starting at 1.45pm</strong>. Students who
are unwell should seek medical attention and not come to school to collect
the results.</p>
<p>3. This is a formal school event. Students are required to wear their
school uniform (Bartley Polo Tee or Beige Uniform with school skirt/pants)
with no accessories and look clean and neat with school approved hairstyle,
length and colour to receive their results.</p>
<p>4. Students who are unable to receive the results personally can appoint
a proxy to collect the results on their behalf. Appointed proxies are requested
to come to school only at 3.00pm. Please proceed to the school’s General
Office to collect the results. Proxies are required to produce the relevant
documents (Photo ID of the proxy and photo ID of the candidate for identification)
for the school’s verification when collecting the results.</p>
<p>5. All students are strongly advised to collect their result on 14 Jan
2026 as they will be required to submit their post-secondary applications
by 19 Jan 2026, 4.30pm.</p>
