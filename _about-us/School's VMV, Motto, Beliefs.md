<h3><strong>Vision</strong></h3>
<p>Disciplined Learners. Principled Leaders. Compassionate Contributors.</p>
<p>
<br>
</p>
<h3><strong>Mission</strong></h3>
<p><em>Building a caring community where everyone can thrive and is anchored by courage, optimism, respect and excellence</em>
</p>
<h3><strong>Values</strong></h3>
<p><em>Courage, Optimism, Respect, Excellence</em>
</p>
<h3><strong>Our Beliefs</strong></h3>
<p>We believe that every child can and will learn and succeed, regardless
of his/her starting point.</p>
<p>We believe that every child is special and has talents, and we can bring
out the best in every child.</p>
<p>We believe a disciplined and orderly environment is necessary before learning
can take place.</p>
<p>We believe a strong home-school partnership will contribute greatly to
the child's success in school.</p>
<h3><strong>Our Commitment</strong></h3>
<p>Provide opportunities to stretch all students
<br>Strict emphasis on discipline
<br>Establish a safe and dynamic environment to grow and learn
<br>Create strong home-school partnership</p>
